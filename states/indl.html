Welcome to Delhi!


Delhi, India's capital, embodies a vibrant blend of traditions and modernity. Its culture celebrates diverse festivals like Diwali, Holi, Eid, and Christmas, reflecting its secular ethos. The city's cuisine, from Mughlai delights like Butter Chicken to street food staples like Chaat and Golgappa, offers a rich culinary experience. Delhi's arts scene thrives with theaters, galleries, and cultural festivals like the Delhi International Arts Festival. Historical landmarks such as the Red Fort and Qutub Minar coexist with modern marvels like the Lotus Temple, showcasing its rich heritage and dynamic growth. Delhi's people, known for their resilience and cosmopolitan spirit, contribute to its vibrant cultural tapestry, making it a city where tradition meets innovation.
